Fly your Prayer Flags in Pharping.

Your flags will be empowered with mantras by experienced meditators and then hung in Pharping in the Water Tiger 2022 Tibetan New Year (Losar). We will take pictures of the flag hoisting which will be then be sent to you by email.

High Quality Prayer Flags.

The flags all are of the most durable material available, teri-cotton, and the print is very resistant.

Fly your lungta for Tibetan culture!

Our aim is to raise money to ensure the continuation of Tibetan culture at this critical time, while at the same time giving practitioners the opportunity to buy lungta prayer flags for the upcoming Losar (Tibetan New Year).

This is a special and rare opportunity to have one's flags blessed by a spiritual master and hung in the holy place of Pharping, Nepal. Buying flags you are contributing towards the activities of the Shang Shung Uk, to see our past activities please CLICK HERE.
