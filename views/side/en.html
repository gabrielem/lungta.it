The hoisting of the flags in Pharping.

The flags will be put up in Pharping on Tibetan Losar and we will take pictures of the flags which will be sent to you by email.

Fly your lungta for Tibetan culture!

Our aim is to raise money to ensure the continuation of Tibetan culture at a critical time while at the same time giving practitioners the opportunity to buy lungta prayer flags for the upcoming Losar (Tibetan New Year).

This is a special and rare opportunity to have one's flags hung up in the very holy place of Pharping, in eastern Nepal and also to contribute money to a worthy cause.
